Administration of E-Placement system

Administration of E-Placement System is web based application that will implement in any college.Every student register with software will have individual login ID and profile in which all personal details, educational qualifications, professional skills, etc. information is stored. Based on companies criteria students are shortlisted and an email or message is send to shortlisted student on their mobiles. The job details of placed students will be provided by the Training and placement officer
